# Hospital-Performance-DashBoard
1. Project Title / HeadLine: Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard – Power BI Project
*Project OverView / Short Description: This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of hospital emergency room (ER) performance to help management and healthcare teams make data-driven decisions.
It focuses on patient flow, wait times, satisfaction, demographics, and referral patterns, providing actionable insights to improve overall service efficiency and patient experience.

2. Purpose:
To optimize hospital operations by reducing patient wait times, improving satisfaction, and enabling data-based decisions for better healthcare service management.
 Business Problem
   Hospitals often struggle to:
   Monitor real-time patient admissions and referrals effectively.
   Identify peak hours and demographic patterns in patient visits. 
   Reduce average wait times and missed target cases.
   Improve patient satisfaction and service quality using data insights.

3. Goal of the Dashboard
The primary goal is to:
Track key ER metrics like number of patients, wait time, satisfaction rate, and referral data.
Visualize trends by age, gender, race, and department to understand patient demographics.
Analyze time-based trends to optimize staffing and resource allocation.
Enhance hospital operations and improve decision-making efficiency through data visualization.  

4. Features & Highlights

 Dynamic Visuals: Interactive cards for KPIs like total patients, average wait time, satisfaction rate, and referral count.
 Performance Metrics: Tracks % of patients seen within 30 minutes and average waiting times.
 Demographic Insights: Visualizes patient data by age group, gender, and race.
 Referral Analysis: Shows patient referrals by department to identify key contributors.
 Time-Based Patterns: Highlights daily and hourly patient inflow trends.
 Monthly & Consolidated Views: Easy navigation between detailed monthly reports and yearly summaries using buttons.

5. Dashboard Walkthrough
1️⃣ Monthly View

Shows a snapshot of hospital performance for a specific month.
KPIs:
Total Patients – 513
Avg Wait Time – 36.3 minutes
Patient Satisfaction – 4.96
Referred Patients – 214
Age group 0–9 has the highest patient count.
61.6% of patients were seen within 30 minutes.
Most patients came during Monday mornings (00–02 hrs).

2️⃣ Consolidated View (May 2023 – Oct 2024)

Tracks performance trends across multiple months.
KPIs:
Total Patients – 8,286
Avg Wait Time – 35.4 minutes
Patient Satisfaction – 4.95
Referred Patients – 3,404
40% of patients missed the target seen time.
Balanced gender ratio with 51% females and 49% males.
Most referrals from General Practice and Orthopedics departments.

Key Insights

Average wait time remains around 35–36 minutes, showing consistent patient handling.
High patient satisfaction score (≈4.9) indicates strong service quality.
Peak visits occur on Mondays and afternoons, requiring better staffing allocation.
Majority of patients belong to the 30–39 age group and White ethnicity.
General Practice department drives the most referrals, showing dependency on general consultations.

Business Impact

✅ Improved resource allocation during peak hours.
✅ Enhanced patient experience by identifying wait-time bottlenecks.
✅ Supported data-backed decisions for staffing and scheduling.
✅ Strengthened operational efficiency and reduced missed service targets.
